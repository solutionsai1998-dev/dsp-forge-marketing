Privacy Policy

DSP Forge
Effective Date: April 15, 2026

1. Introduction

DSP Forge ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our hours tracking and scheduling platform ("Service").

This Policy applies to:

  • DSP owners and managers who create accounts ("Administrators")
  • Drivers and employees whose information is stored in the system ("Workers")

By using the Service, you consent to the practices described in this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Administrator Accounts:

  • Business name and contact information
  • Email address and phone number
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe; we don't store full card numbers)

Worker Information (entered by Administrators):

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Job role and qualifications
  • Assigned delivery station

Operational Data:

  • Hours worked and scheduled shifts
  • Attendance records (check-ins, check-outs)
  • Break times
  • Time-off requests and approvals

Automatically Collected:

  • Login timestamps and IP addresses
  • Browser type and device information
  • Usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Error logs for troubleshooting

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

Provide the Service:

  • Enable hours tracking and schedule management
  • Generate attendance reports
  • Send shift reminders and notifications
  • Process payments and billing

Improve and Maintain:

  • Fix bugs and improve performance
  • Analyze usage to develop new features
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized access

Communicate:

  • Send service-related notifications
  • Respond to support requests
  • Share important updates about the Service

We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Share information with Amazon (we're an independent service)

4. How We Store and Protect Data

Hosting: Our Service is hosted on Railway, a cloud infrastructure provider with data centers in the United States. All data is stored on secure PostgreSQL databases with encryption at rest.

Security Measures:

  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt
  • All data transmission uses TLS/SSL encryption
  • Database connections are encrypted
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access limited to authorized personnel only

Data Retention:

  • Active accounts: Data is retained while your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: We retain data for 60 days after account closure, then permanently delete it
  • Backups: Encrypted backups are retained for 30 days for disaster recovery
  • Legal compliance: We may retain data longer if required by law

No Guarantees: While we take security seriously, no online service is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Sharing Your Information

We share information only in limited circumstances:

Service Providers:

  • Railway: Hosting and database infrastructure
  • Stripe: Payment processing (your payment details go directly to Stripe; we only receive confirmation)
  • Email providers: For sending notifications and support communications

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to comply with legal process, protect our rights, prevent fraud, or respond to government requests.

Business Transfers: If DSP Forge is acquired or merges with another company, user data may be transferred to the new owner. We'll notify you of any such change.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Administrators:

  • Access and export all data associated with your account
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete worker data (immediately removes from active use; permanently deleted per retention schedule)
  • Cancel your account at any time

Workers: Because accounts are business-owned, Workers should contact their employer (the Administrator) to access, correct, or delete their personal data. We will assist Workers directly only if the Administrator is unresponsive or to comply with legal requirements.

Data Export: Administrators can request a full export of their account data by emailing solutions.ai1998@gmail.com. We'll provide the data in a common format (CSV or JSON) within 5 business days.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

8. International Data Transfers

All data is stored and processed in the United States. If you access the Service from outside the US, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in the US.

9. California Privacy Rights

If you're a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know: Request disclosure of what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Right to delete: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal exceptions)
  • Right to opt out: We don't sell data, so there's nothing to opt out of
  • Right to non-discrimination: We won't discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, email solutions.ai1998@gmail.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
